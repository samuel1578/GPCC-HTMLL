Apostle Henry Ampomah-Boateng

Founder & Chairman — Global Peace Christian Centre (GPCC)

Overview

  • Education: BSc (Hons) Building Technology, UST/KNUST (1986–1990)
  • Call to Ministry: Sept 1990 (37 University Hall, Katanga – KNUST)
  • Fellowship Start: June 1996
  • GPCC Registered: Nov 2003
  • Church Commenced: Sept 2013 (first building ready at ABLOR-ADJEI, Ga East, Accra)
  • Global Launch (Vision): Sept 2032
2 Corinthians 3:17
...and where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom. (NIV)

Biography Timeline

1986–1990

Studied Building Technology (BSc Hons) at UST (now KNUST), Kumasi.

September 1990

Called by the Lord on the final day of school at 37 University Hall, Katanga (KNUST).

Premier book: “Jesus Walks Into My Room” summarizes this calling.

June–August 1994

Encounters with the Lord led toward the start of fellowship.

June 1996

Started fellowship: PEACE-DOM IN CHRIST PRAYER MINISTRY / FREEDOM IN CHRIST CHURCH.

November 2003

Registration of Global Peace Christian Centre (GPCC).

September 2013

Commencement of the church per the Lord’s word; first church building was ready in ABLOR-ADJEI (Ga East, Accra).

Habakkuk 2:3
For the vision is yet for an appointed time; though it tarries, wait for it; it will surely come.
2023

Revelation: the Global Ministry is to be launched in September 2032.

September 2032 (Vision)

Global Ministry Launch of the Global Peace Christian Ministry worldwide.

Matthew 24:14
