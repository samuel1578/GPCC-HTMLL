IT HAS INCREASED MY PRAYER LIFE

As a Christian it was always a challenge for me to pray more than 30 minutes, I had always yearned to increase the number of hours I prayed, however, the desire and motivation was never there.

After reading the 'Chapter 2' of the book which dwelt on the calling of Pastor by the Lord Jesus Christ, as well as Chapter five, "Baptism of the Holy Spirit", I focussed my prayer life on Acts 1:8.

"But you will receive power when the holy spirit comes on you and you will be my witness in Jerusalem and Judea and Samaria and all the ends of the earth..."

I have since had a transformational encounter with the Lord and can now pray for 2 hours non stop and I have the book 'Jesus Walks Into My Room' to thank for that.

MIRACLE TWIN TESTIMONY

After having my personal encounter from reading the book Jesus Walks into my Room, I decided to recommend it to family and friends so that they can also partake in the blessings I have.

I recommended the book to a cousin who had been married for five(5) years with no issue as a result of several miscarriages. She grabbed the booik with joy and said "This book is written for me". She raid the book three times and placed it on her stomach by faith and prayed to the Lord to visit her as he had visited the man of God, by the grace of God as as the time of writing this testimony she is pregnant with twins.

I quote her "Ever since you gave me the book, I was enticed by the title and I told myself "Jesus will indeed touch my stomach"; and indeed my miscarriages are now over, I was pregnant after 5 years of my marriage beginning in pain".

MIRACLE NATURAL DELIVERY

"This is not the patient I arranged for Caesarean Section!!" The Doctor screams out in the theatre.

I introduced the same book to a friend who was pregnant and was due for a Caesarean Section. On that day, she asked that the book be given to her to take to the theatre. She put the book right beside her and prayed a prayer of faith. As soon as the doctor got to the theatre he screamed 'this is not the patient I arranged for CS'. She gave birth through natural delivery, Indeed this is the doing of the Lord and it is marvellous in our sight.

A MIRACLE OF SURVIVAL

By: Elder Alexander Gogoe

A testimony by Mr Alexander Gogoe, The Nothern Regional Director of the Department of Urban Roads. He experienced the touch of God upon his life through a mighty deliverance he received under the unction of the anointed man of God, Pastor Henry Ampomah-Boateng at the Church Auditorium of the Global Peace Christian Centre on Friday the 18th of November 2016. He gives a vivid account:

Prior to this fateful day I recall that about six(6) to eight(8) months earlier I started feeling some sporadic sharp pains within the upper part of my chest exactly behind the chest muscle. The pain gradually grew in intensity day after day until I couldn't breathe freely.

"He then instructed me to rise up and stand by my bed bare footed with my chest out like a soldier. He advised me to see myself as a soldier in the army of the Lord."

After the prayer session he requested me to inhale and exhale five times and asked how I was feeling. To God be the glory and for a truth the excruciating pain had vanished. He then told me he would check on me in the afternoon to see how I was faring. Suddenly I started jumping in my room to the amazement of my family and doing all sorts of excercices till I began sweating profusely amidst shouts of joy, praise, worship and thanksgiving to God.

I suddenly felt refreshed within my system. My breathing normalized. I became very light as I could tell that some heavy load or burden had been lifted of me. The pressure on my rib cage and lungs had vanished.

I felt a new being within me. When I went back to post in Tamale all who saw me testified that they could see a new me. Some even went to the extent of taking pictures of me because they claim they could see a radiance on my face and around me.

I am greatful to God Almighty for his grace upon my life, and my family. I am equally grateful for the selfless and humble servant Pastor Henry Ampomah-Boateng, his wife, the leadership and the entire congregation of GPCC. I say that "May the good Lord reward your sacrifices for thy Kingdom, and may he continue to fill your hearts with his kind of peace that surpasses all understanding now and forevermore.

I say to this to the glory of God I am healed in Jesus Name Amen

TESTIMONY FROM EMMANUEL MANIGAR

My name is Emmanuel Manigar, a medical student (at the time) at the University of Development Studies, Tamale and a proud member of Global Peace Christian Centre(GPCC).

On 10th April 2017, an unfortunate incident happened when I was training with my college football team ahead of a match between my class and one of the junior undergraduates, during the session the football hit my manhood and I felt severe pains thereafter.

"Pastor prayed with me and told me the healing will be manifested by midnight and there will be no pain at all."

At exactly midnight, the power of God was present. I felt again, the power of God upon me just like how I felt when he prayed with me in the church on Saturday and the pain left. Honestly the miracle manifested as he said and I received the healing.

The power in my manhood had been restored and I started experiencing normal erections as before the incident happened. On Tuesday April,2017 I went to see the Urologist(Specialist) at Tamale but he was not available, I therefore came down to Accra and went to Achimota Hospital on Thursday 20th April,2017 to do an ultrasonography study of the scrotum and the report confirmed that everything was normal with my manhood, Thank God for Healing Me.

THE POWER OF GOD SHOOK OUT ADDICTION

FROM: DAVID K BOATENG

Isiah 10:27..."It shall come to pass in that day that his burden will be taken away from your shoulder and his yoke from your neck, and the yoke will be destroyed because of the anointing oil."

My name is David K Boateng. I testify to the glory of God what God has done in my father in-laws life.

My father in-law was so addicted to alcohol, to the extent that immediately he woke up early in the morning, he headed for the nearest drinking bar. On one of my visits to Accra, I stopped by at Awutu Breku to visit him but when I saw him I was vexed in my spirit and wept because of his state.

Two weeks later I was in Accra to visit my spiritual father- Rev. Ampomah-Boateng.

I brought some olive oil and asked Rev. Ampomah-Boateng to pray over it for me, but I didn't mention my father in-laws condition to him. He prayed over the oil and asked me to shake it whenever I wanted to use it.

As soon as I got home my wife and I arranged to bring my father in-law to Cape Coast, where we stayed. He was in a very pathetic state. Immediately he arrived I took water to the bathroom for him, brought out the anointing oil that Rev Ampomah-Boateng had prayed over, shook the oil and prayed my heart out to the Lord. "God your servant instructed me to shake the oil before using it; I declare that as I shake it O God to anoint the water my father in-law will bath with, SHAKE OUT EVERY ADDICTION OF ALCOHOLISM OFF HIM IN THE NAME OF JESUS".

Brethren since that day to the Glory of God he has stopped taking alcohol, eats very well and looks very healthy. God is merciful and wonderful.

UNCOMMON LIFT HOME AFTER EVENING SERVICE

It was one of our usual Friday evening prayer meetings, and Head Pastor taught on the subject of excercising faith. I held on to the message till the service ended. It was 9:36pm when I got to the junction that led to my house-a distance of about 500m.

The last taxi at the station had just driven off as I got close. This naturally left me stranded, Holding on to the Head Pastors message which says everything is possible to them that believe; I said to the Lord, "please take me home".

No sooner had I spoken to the Lord, than a woman in her mid fifties drove from the opposite direction and picked me up in her car. She instructed me as she passed by me the Lord told her to pick me but she ignored it, She heard the Lord's voice again to come back and pick me up and she ignored it the second time, She said she caould not ignore the voice anymore as it kept sounding in her ears and she finally had to make a turn to pick me up at the junction and drop me off at home.

She wanted to know where I fellowship and I told her Global Peace Christian Centre at Abokobi (Ablor-Adjei). She nodded her head in a thoughtful manner and repeated the name of the church twice to herself. That is how the Lord took me home because I believed it was possible to them that believe it was possible.

God richly bless you Very Rev. Henry Ampomah-Boateng for your message on that fateful Friday which taught me that indeed, everything is possible to them that believe.

Mark 9:23 - If you can believe, all things are possibe to him who believes.

THE LORD DELIVERED MY LITTLE GIRL FROM CHRONIC STOMACH PAINS AND HEADACHES

This is what the Lord has done for me. My name is Seth and at the time of writing this I had a 10 year old daughter, She started complaining about severe headaches and stomach pains.

She fainted at school one afternoon and got admitted to 37 Military Hospital.

This became our routine for two or three months, she would be discharged from the hospital and in less than a week we will rush back. We told our Pastor about it and a deliverance prayer session was organized for my family. It was there the evil spirits responsible for her situation were cast out. Since then, she has not been admitted at the 37 Military hospital again. I give thanks to the Lord for the deliverance of my little girl.
